# Free Online Invoice Generator

A professional, free online invoice generator designed for freelancers, small businesses, and contractors worldwide. Create, customize, and export beautiful invoices in minutes.

## Features

### Core Features
- **Professional Invoice Templates** - 3 beautifully designed templates (Classic, Modern, Minimal)
- **Multi-Currency Support** - 25+ currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, BRL, and more
- **PDF Export** - Download invoices as professional PDF documents
- **Print-Ready** - Optimized print layout for physical copies
- **Auto-Calculations** - Automatic totals, tax, and discount calculations
- **Logo Upload** - Add your company logo to invoices
- **Invoice Number Auto-Generation** - Smart invoice numbering system
- **Save & Load** - LocalStorage-based invoice management
- **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes for late-night invoicing
- **Responsive Design** - Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile

### Privacy & Security
- **100% Client-Side** - No data sent to servers
- **No Registration Required** - Start creating invoices immediately
- **Local Storage Only** - Your data stays on your device

### Business Features
- **Multiple Invoice Statuses** - Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue
- **Payment Terms** - Due on Receipt, Net 15/30/45/60
- **Discount Support** - Percentage or fixed amount discounts
- **Line Item Tax** - Per-item tax rates
- **Notes Section** - Add payment instructions or terms

## Target Audience

- **Freelancers** - Designers, developers, writers, consultants
- **Small Businesses** - Startups, agencies, local businesses
- **Contractors** - Independent professionals, gig workers
- **International Users** - Multi-currency support for global business

## How to Use

### Step 1: Enter Your Company Details
1. Upload your company logo (optional)
2. Enter your company name, address, email, and phone
3. These details will appear on the invoice header

### Step 2: Add Client Information
1. Enter the client's name and company
2. Add their billing address and email
3. This populates the "Bill To" section

### Step 3: Configure Invoice Settings
1. Choose your preferred template (Classic, Modern, or Minimal)
2. Select your currency from 25+ options
3. Set the invoice status (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue)
4. Enter invoice number (auto-generated) and dates
5. Choose payment terms (Net 30, etc.)

### Step 4: Add Line Items
1. Click "Add Line Item" for each service/product
2. Enter description, quantity, rate, and tax percentage
3. Totals calculate automatically
4. Add discount if needed (percentage or fixed)

### Step 5: Add Notes (Optional)
1. Include payment instructions
2. Add terms and conditions
3. Write a thank-you note

### Step 6: Export Your Invoice
1. **Export PDF** - Download as a professional PDF file
2. **Print** - Print directly from the browser
3. **Save** - Store invoice locally for future reference
4. **Load** - Retrieve previously saved invoices
